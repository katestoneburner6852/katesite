Social Media Strategy

I currently oversee social media strategy and content creation on Facebook, Instagram, Snapchat, LinkedIn and Twitter, and even dabble in TikTok. I take the lead project-managing when content needs include videography and photography. I'm also passionate about brand consistency. That means presenting brand leaders with data-based reasons to follow best practices and unify across platforms with imagery and tone that is recognizably "theirs."

Publications and Writing

I love to write, and I take on everything from webpage copy to magazine features to engaging alt text. My favorite projects are those that require me to listen and learn from others. It is deeply satisfying to me when I get to take a piece from start to finish, from interviewing to editing (and editing, and editing). No matter the platform, I know from experience that timely, accurate and engaging written word can be key to maintaining a tight-knit community and helping campaigns succeed.
